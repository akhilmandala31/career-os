## How To Learn

For every topic ask:

1. What is it?
2. Why does it exist?
3. What problem does it solve?
4. What alternatives exist?
5. What breaks at scale?
6. How do we use it at GAP?

---

## Architecture Thinking (Cross-Cutting)

Apply to every topic, every tier — not a separate phase:

- Why does this exist?
- What alternatives were considered?
- What breaks at scale?
- What's the cost of being wrong?
- Would you still choose it at 10x?
